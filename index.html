FAQ

Does Mise work without internet on set?

Yes. Mise is offline-first: every tool works with no signal, and changes sync automatically to your crew when you reconnect.

Do my crew members all need subscriptions?

Pricing is per device — $4.99/month for the first device and $2.99/month for each additional one, far below the $29–99/month of typical production platforms.

Can I use Mise without creating an account?

Yes. Mise works fully locally without signing in. An account is only needed for multi-device sync and crew collaboration.

What can I export?

Call sheets, budgets, and reports export to PDF or CSV, and anything can go out through your device's native share sheet.

What does AI Import do with my data?

It converts what you type, paste, or photograph into structured shot lists, crew sheets, and notes. Your data is never sold, never used to train AI, and never shared with advertisers — see the privacy policy.
